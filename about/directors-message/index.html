Director's Message

C. Muhamed Nizar
CEO, Qatar Multitech

Qatar Multi-Tech is a leading supplier and service provider in Telecom Engineering, Security & Access Control systems, Industrial Power Management and Network Engineering catering to both corporate and consumer market.

The company is committed to the implementation, maintenance and continuous monitoring of the quality management system that it believes is the only means by which exemplary results can be delivered. This is achieved by giving attention to every minute detail of the company through planning, controlling, reviewing and improving all our processes.

Multi-Tech always stands by its vision- to make the best technologies from around the world accessible to markets in Qatar and the Middle-East.
