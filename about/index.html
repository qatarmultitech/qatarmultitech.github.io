About Us

Since our beginning in 1995, Qatar Multi-Tech has been scaling uphill in every sector.

We strive to achieve the vision we set for ourselves.

"Bring latest technologies to the region and build Qatar Multi-Tech into an internationally renowned brand"

Multitech is now expanding its service line up to cater sectors such as Data Center Management and Renewable Energy Sector.
